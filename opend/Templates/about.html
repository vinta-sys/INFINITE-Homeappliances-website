Welcome to INFINITE an eCommerce website

INFINITE Home appliances,Furniture & Decors HomeStore is the #1 selling furniture brand in the world and the #1 retailer of furniture and Decors in the United States. With a rich history dating back to 1945, INFINITE has gone from humble beginnings as a Wisconsin-based furniture manufacturer to a global powerhouse for furniture retail. Today, INFINITE operates in 6,000 retail outlets located in 123 countries across the globe. INFINITE Furniture has one of the widest selections of furniture available to shop. With over 75 years of expertise in building highly quality furniture for everyday living, INFINITE offers complete solutions for living, dining, bedroom, kitchen, home office, accessories and outdoors. We are passionate about every piece of furniture we build, paying close attention to every detail. From the moment we conceive of a design, to the point that our furniture is produced in our world class manufacturing operations, and ultimately packaged, delivered and accepted into the home, we aim to satisfy families and individuals who desire enduring style at home. Looking forward to seeing you at our new showroom, to experience a new lifestyle of comfort and quality - the American way. This INFINITE Furniture Homestore website and stores are independently owned and locally operated by VINTAMART SOLUTIONS PVT LTD, holder of an INFINITE license in Coimbatore and Kochi.
